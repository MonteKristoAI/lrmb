## The ten content gaps nobody is filling

These are the specific topics where the SERP is thin or nobody has a definitive answer. Every gap below is worth a post, and several of them are worth two.

1. **Edibles that actually feel like smoking a joint.** The entire first page of Google is brand posts dancing around the question. Nobody has a yes answer with science behind it.
2. **A plain-English pharmacokinetics story.** Everyone explains nano-emulsification badly. A definitive explainer with real diagrams becomes the canonical reference the category links to.
3. **Terpene profile matched to outcome.** Limonene for creative work. Linalool for sleep. Myrcene for relaxation. Nobody ships product lines with content that maps terpene to moment.
4. **A current state-by-state legality tracker.** Most ranking pages are 2023 or 2024. The legal picture has shifted since then and nobody has refreshed.
5. **Microdosing for performance.** "Microdose gummies" is owned by mushroom brands. No hemp brand has claimed the sessionable-low-dose THC positioning.
6. **"How do I know if edibles are actually working for me."** Zero authoritative answers. This is a tool-worthy post, potentially with an onset-time calculator as a linkable asset.
7. **Hemp gummies for people who do not want to be knocked out.** The negative framing is a huge buyer objection with zero branded answer.
8. **"Ediblocked." The real reason edibles stop working for some people.** Reddit calls it ediblocked. No brand has a plain-language post explaining the enzyme story behind it.
9. **First-time buyer path paired with low-dose SKU.** The category over-indexes on 25mg "knockout" framing. The beginner path is wide open.
10. **Occasion-based content.** Gummies for a concert. Gummies for a dinner party. Gummies for a long hike. Long-tail ritual content nobody has claimed.

Every one of these gaps lines up with something Entourage Gummies can credibly deliver. We are not inventing narratives. We are claiming narratives the category has left on the table.
