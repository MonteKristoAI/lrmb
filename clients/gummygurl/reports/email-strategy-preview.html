Compliance

Klaviyo supports hemp/CBD businesses (Mailchimp bans them). Every email includes:

  • 21+ age disclaimer
  • FDA disclaimer (not evaluated, not intended to diagnose/treat/cure)
  • No medical claims in any email copy
  • Easy one-click unsubscribe
  • CAN-SPAM compliant physical address
