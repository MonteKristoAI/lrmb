METHODOLOGY (THE AIIACO METHOD)

Phase 1. Find the Friction
   Process mapping, bottleneck identification, coordination audit, and data reliability checks across all departments. AiiACo maps how work flows through the business, where tasks wait on people, where admins chase updates, and where coordination is consuming margin.

Phase 2. Implement the Fix
   Deploy workflow automation, CRM reactivation systems, and outreach automation. Implementation of practical AI systems that eliminate the specific friction points identified in the audit phase.

Phase 3. Measure the Improvement
   Build real-time dashboards, establish execution speed metrics, and create revenue visibility reporting. Every module is measured against KPIs defined before implementation begins.

Phase 4. Expand What Works
   Cross-department rollout of proven modules, playbook documentation, and team training. Scale the systems that demonstrate measurable results across the entire business.
