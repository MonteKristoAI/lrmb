## Tone and Style Guide (All Pages)

- Authority-driven, direct, zero fluff
- No em dashes anywhere. Use hyphens, commas, colons, or split into two sentences
- No "cutting-edge," "game-changer," "revolutionize," "seamlessly," "harness the power of," "leverage" (as verb), "dive into," "navigate the landscape"
- First paragraph of every H2 is a direct answer with a statistic and source
- Active voice default. Passive only when the actor is genuinely unknown
- Contractions are fine ("it's," "we've," "don't")
- Sentence target: 15-20 words. Mix short (8 words) and longer (25 words) for rhythm
- Paragraph target: 40-80 words. Hard ceiling: 150 words
- No promotional language. Educational tone. Let the infrastructure speak for itself
