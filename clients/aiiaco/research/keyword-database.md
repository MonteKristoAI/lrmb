# AiiACo Keyword Database v2 (Ultra-Detailed)

**Tier definitions (refined for v2)**:

- **Platinum** (92 kws): KD at most 20, volume at least 40. Zero-competition steals. These are the first 30 posts to ship.
- **Gold** (33 kws): KD 21-30, volume at least 50. Winnable within 90 days once a few posts rank.
- **Silver** (48 kws): KD 31-45, volume at least 80. Phase 2 targets once domain authority builds.
- **Bronze** (100 kws): KD 46-60, volume at least 30. Long-term authority plays, mostly spokes in clusters.

## Top 10 findings

1. **92 Platinum keywords is 4x what a typical B2B consulting blog finds in research**. The category is under-indexed because no operator publishes practitioner content. AiiACo can claim 20-30 Platinum keywords in the first 90 days.
2. **AI Overview present on 9 of 15 spot-checked queries (60%)**. Every Phase 1 post must be structured for passage citation, not just organic rank. Reddit appears in top 10 on 6 of 15 queries - plain-language Q&A format wins.
3. **People Also Ask present on 8 of 15 queries**. Each PAA question is a free FAQ schema input. Harvest the 30+ PAA questions collected in section 'SERP Feature Inventory' and bake them into post FAQ sections.
4. **AI SDR cluster is the deepest vein** (67 keywords). Volume range 30 to 2400. KD range 0 to 45. Dominated by vendors (aisdr.com owns 784 ranked keywords per DataForSEO competitor scan) but AI Overview cites framework content, not product pages.
5. **Consulting cluster is completely unclaimed** (35 keywords, many KD 0). 'ai consultants for small business' KD 0 vol 390. 'ai agent for small business' KD 0 vol 260. These are publish-and-win targets.
6. **Real estate cluster**: 53 Platinum/Gold/Silver keywords. NAR owns the head term but every CRM-specific query (Follow Up Boss, kvCORE, BoomTown) is wide open.
7. **Mortgage cluster has low KD + advisory-heavy SERP**. Mortgage AI KD 4 vol 320 but top ranks are StratmorGroup (485 ranked kws), MBA, NationalMortgageProfessional. Practitioner voice is absent.
8. **Workflow automation cluster** (46 keywords) is owned by Gumloop + n8n for head terms, but platform-agnostic comparison content ranks on 'best ai workflow automation tools' (480 vol KD 25).
9. **Governance cluster is thin** (2 keywords) but strategic. EU AI Act compliance (140 vol KD 21) is a trust-moat topic - ship defensibility content here in Phase 2.
10. **Vacation rental cluster** (9 keywords) is platform-locked (Guesty + Hostaway dominate) but 'ai for property management' 320 vol KD 13 is winnable with an integrator-voice post.
