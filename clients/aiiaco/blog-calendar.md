## Internal Linking Strategy

Each post follows a hub-and-spoke model:

- **Pillar posts** (2,500+ words) link to all supporting posts in their cluster and to the corresponding service page.
- **Supporting posts** (1,500-2,000 words) link up to their pillar post, across to 1-2 posts in adjacent pillars, and to their service page.
- **Industry posts** link to the relevant industry service page plus 2-3 operational/CRM/revenue posts that apply to that vertical.
- **Every post** includes a contextual link to the homepage and at least one other service page outside its own pillar.

## Writing Guidelines (AiiAco-Specific)

1. **Answer first.** Every post opens with a direct, factual answer in the first 50 words. No preambles, no "in this article we will explore."
2. **No fluff.** Every paragraph earns its place with data, a framework, or a concrete example. Cut anything that restates the obvious.
3. **No em dashes.** Use commas, hyphens, colons, or split into two sentences.
4. **AEO formatting.** H2s are questions. First paragraph under each H2 is a standalone answer an AI system could extract and cite.
5. **Industry grounding.** Even infrastructure/CRM/operations posts should include at least one example from real estate, vacation rentals, or service businesses.
6. **Internal links.** Every post links to its pillar, at least one adjacent pillar post, and the relevant service page. No orphan posts.
7. **Stats with sources.** Minimum 8 sourced statistics per pillar post, 4-6 per supporting post. Named sources only.
8. **Founder authority.** Nemr Hallak's perspective can appear as expert commentary (1-2 instances per post maximum, author bio context only).
