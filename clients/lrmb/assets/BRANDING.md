## Design Style
- **Tone:** Professional, luxury, aspirational
- **Energy:** Medium - calm confidence
- **Color scheme:** Light (white background)
- **Border radius:** 5px (subtle rounding)
- **Inputs:** 20px border radius (pill-shaped)
- **Overall feel:** High-end hospitality, clean lines, warm neutrals with ocean teal accents
