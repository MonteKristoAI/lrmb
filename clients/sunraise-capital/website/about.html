Founder & CEO

Nathan Jovanelly

Nathan founded SunRaise Capital to solve a structural problem he witnessed firsthand: the residential solar market generates strong unit economics, but the capital stack that funds it remains fragmented, opaque, and misaligned. Installers compete on dealer fees rather than system quality. Capital providers lack visibility into the assets they fund. Homeowners navigate a process designed around channel economics, not their interests.

Before SunRaise, Nathan led the build-out of IGS Solar's residential division starting in 2016, growing it to 150MW and $600M in managed assets. Over his career, he has been directly involved in deploying more than $3 billion into solar energy infrastructure across residential, commercial, and utility-scale segments.

A licensed Chemical Engineer (P.E.) by training, Nathan brings an engineer's discipline to financial structuring. He designed SunRaise's origination engine to embed the capital provider's required return directly into pricing at the point of sale, eliminating the downstream misalignment that creates risk in conventional models.

He currently serves on the Board of SunVena Solar, one of the fastest-growing residential solar installers in the Southeast. At SunRaise, he is building a distributed platform that gives capital providers, installers, and homeowners a shared system of record with transparent governance across the full asset lifecycle.

Principles

What Drives Us

Six operating principles that govern every decision, from origination through asset management.

Alignment First

Every participant in the solar value chain should benefit from the same transaction. We structure deals so capital providers, installers, and homeowners share aligned incentives from origination through the full asset lifecycle.

Institutional Discipline

We apply the rigor of institutional asset management to residential solar. Required returns are embedded at origination, not approximated after the fact. Every asset is governed by the same underwriting standards regardless of installer or market.

Radical Transparency

Capital providers see real-time asset performance. Installers see clear funding criteria. Homeowners see exactly what they pay and what they save. Opacity creates risk. Transparency eliminates it.

Technology-Enabled

Software is the enforcement layer. Our platform automates pricing governance, compliance checks, billing, collections, and performance reporting so the rules hold at scale without manual intervention.

Scalable Integrity

Doing the right thing should not break at volume. We designed every process, from installer onboarding to homeowner servicing, to maintain the same standard of care whether we manage 100 assets or 100,000.

Long-term Orientation

Solar assets perform over 25 years. We build relationships, technology, and financial structures on that same timeline. Short-term margin grabs destroy long-term asset quality. We optimize for durability.
