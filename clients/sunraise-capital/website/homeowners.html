Your Savings

Your Savings Grow Every Year

Your utility company raises rates every year. On average, electricity prices go up 3-5% annually, and in some states much faster. That means the amount you pay keeps climbing with no end in sight.

With SunRaise, your solar rate starts 10-20% below what you pay today, and it only increases by a fixed 2.99% each year. Because utility rates rise faster than your solar rate, the gap between what you would have paid and what you actually pay gets wider every year.

That widening gap is money that stays in your pocket.

Questions Homeowners Ask

Your solar system will generate most or all of the electricity your home needs. You will still receive a utility bill, but it will typically show only a small connection fee. Your SunRaise payment replaces the majority of what you used to pay your utility company, and it starts lower from day one. Most homeowners see 10-20% savings immediately.

Your solar agreement transfers to the new homeowner at the time of sale. Since the new owner inherits built-in savings on their electricity, solar can actually make your home more attractive to buyers. Homes with solar panels sell faster and for more money on average, according to multiple national housing studies.
