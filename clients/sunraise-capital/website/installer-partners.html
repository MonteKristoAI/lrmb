Growth & Scale

Scale Without the Risk

Growing your installation volume should not mean growing your exposure to a single capital source. SunRaise gives you the infrastructure to scale sustainably.

Diversified Capital

Access multiple institutional capital sources through a single platform. If one fund adjusts its appetite, your pipeline keeps moving through alternative channels.

No Concentration Risk

Stop depending on a single lender for your business. SunRaise distributes your deal flow across capital providers, protecting you from market disruptions.

Volume Incentives

As your installation volume grows, SunRaise pricing improves. High-performing installers with strong asset quality unlock better rates and priority underwriting.

Optional Model

Retain Ownership of Your Assets

For qualified installers, SunRaise offers an optional project ownership model. Rather than selling every asset to a third party, retain equity in the systems you install and build long-term recurring revenue.

Recurring Revenue Stream

Build a portfolio of owned assets that generate monthly cash flow over the life of each system.

SunRaise Handles Operations

Billing, collections, customer support, and asset management are all handled by SunRaise. You own the asset without the back-office burden.

Enterprise Valuation Uplift

A portfolio of owned, cash-flowing solar assets increases your company's enterprise value and opens refinancing options as you scale.
