Pricing Engine

IRR-Embedded Pricing

Most solar financing platforms set pricing based on market competition, then retrofit returns for capital providers. SunRaise inverts this process entirely.

Capital provider return targets are solved at origination. The platform's pricing engine calculates the minimum lease or PPA rate required to achieve the target IRR for each specific asset, accounting for geography, system size, utility rates, credit profile, and equipment specifications.

The result is structural alignment: every proposal that reaches a homeowner has already satisfied the capital provider's return requirements. No post-hoc negotiation. No return compression.

Underwriting

Automated, Next-Business-Day Credit Decisions

SunRaise's underwriting engine processes installer submissions through automated credit scoring, compliance verification, and data integrity checks. Compliant proposals receive a credit decision within one business day.

Every submission is reviewed for design accuracy, equipment specifications, utility rate validation, and regulatory compliance before capital is allocated. This standardized process eliminates the delays, inconsistencies, and manual bottlenecks that slow down traditional TPO underwriting.

  • Automated credit scoring with configurable thresholds
  • Standardized documentation templates across all markets
  • QA validation on every asset before capital allocation
  • Real-time status tracking for installers and capital partners
