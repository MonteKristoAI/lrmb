After You Refer

What Happens Next

Our streamlined process ensures patients receive care as quickly as possible.

1

We Review Within 2 Hours

Our admissions team receives your referral and reviews the patient's clinical information to confirm eligibility.

2

We Contact the Patient

A Bridge Hospice coordinator reaches out to the patient and family to discuss care options, answer questions, and schedule an assessment.

3

Care Begins Within 24 Hours

Once the patient elects hospice, our interdisciplinary team begins providing comprehensive care, typically within 24 hours of admission.

Have questions about the referral process? Our admissions team is here to help.
