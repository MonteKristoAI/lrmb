Our Services

End-of-Life Care

Bridge Hospice provides more than 12 specialized services designed to support patients and their families through every stage of the hospice journey, delivered by our interdisciplinary team of 25+ caregivers in Lubbock and the surrounding South Plains.

CHAP Certified 24/7 Care 5x Best of the West
Compassionate & Comprehensive

Care That Surrounds the Whole Family

When a loved one faces a life-limiting illness, every family deserves more than medical treatment. Bridge Hospice delivers an integrated circle of support that addresses physical comfort, emotional well-being, spiritual peace, and practical needs under one coordinated team.

Our interdisciplinary staff of 25+ caregivers brings together physicians, registered nurses, therapists, social workers, chaplains, and trained volunteers. Each member knows your loved one by name and works from a care plan built around your family's goals, not a one-size-fits-all checklist.

What We Provide

Interdisciplinary Care, Tailored to You

Every patient receives a personalized care plan built around their needs, preferences, and goals. Our team coordinates across disciplines to deliver seamless support.

👥

Pre-Admission Consultation

Our care team meets with you and your family before admission to answer every question and explain what hospice truly means. We walk through eligibility, insurance coverage, and what the first days of care will look like so there are no surprises.

📞

24/7 Nursing & Emergency Support

Registered nurses are available around the clock for urgent symptom changes, medication questions, or emotional crises. One phone call connects you to a real member of our care team, day or night, weekends and holidays included.

💊

Pain & Symptom Management

Controlling pain is our first priority. Our clinical team uses the latest protocols to manage pain, nausea, breathing difficulties, and anxiety so your loved one remains comfortable and present for the moments that matter most.

🩹

Physician Medical Director

A board-certified physician oversees every patient's care plan, collaborating with your personal doctor and our nursing staff. The Medical Director reviews treatment adjustments, conducts face-to-face assessments, and ensures clinical excellence across the team.

⚡

Intensive Intervention Care

When symptoms escalate beyond what routine home care can manage, we provide continuous or inpatient-level nursing at the bedside. This intensive support stabilizes the patient and prevents unnecessary hospital visits during critical periods.

💜

Therapist & Counseling Services

Licensed therapists offer individual and family counseling to help process grief, fear, and the emotional weight of a terminal diagnosis. Sessions are held in the home or via telehealth, whichever is more comfortable for your family.

🙏

Social Work & Pastoral Care

Our social workers connect families with community resources, financial assistance, and advance directive planning. Pastoral counselors provide spiritual support that respects every belief system, faith tradition, or personal philosophy.

📚

Pharmacy Consultation

A consulting pharmacist reviews all medications to prevent harmful interactions and ensure the most effective comfort regimen. We coordinate directly with local pharmacies to handle refills and delivery so you never have to worry about prescriptions.

🌿

Bereavement Program

Grief does not end at the time of passing. Our bereavement team provides 13 months of follow-up support including counseling, support groups, memorial events, and resource mailings to help families navigate their loss at their own pace.

🎓

Family & Caregiver Education

We teach families practical caregiving skills, what to expect as conditions change, and how to recognize signs that require a call to our nursing line. Prepared caregivers feel less anxious and more empowered to provide meaningful comfort at home.

🛋

Medical Equipment & Supplies

Hospital beds, oxygen concentrators, wheelchairs, wound care supplies, and incontinence products are delivered directly to your home at no cost. Our team handles all ordering, setup, and equipment adjustments as needs evolve.

🏠

Full Hospice Care Solutions

From the initial referral through bereavement, Bridge Hospice coordinates every aspect of end-of-life care under one roof. Our interdisciplinary team works together so families deal with one trusted partner instead of navigating a fragmented healthcare system alone.

What to Expect

Your Hospice Journey

From first contact to long-term bereavement support, here is what the process looks like when you choose Bridge Hospice.

1

Initial Consultation

A member of our admissions team meets with you and your family, often within 24 hours of your call. We answer every question in plain language so you can make a fully informed decision.

2

Care Plan Development

Our Medical Director, nursing team, social worker, and counselor build a personalized care plan around the patient's diagnosis, symptoms, and family goals.

3

Your Team Arrives

Within 48 hours your assigned nurse, aide, and social worker visit the home to deliver equipment, begin medications, and establish a routine.

4

Ongoing Support

Scheduled visits, counseling sessions, and 24/7 on-call coverage continue for as long as needed. Your care plan is adjusted as conditions change.

5

Bereavement & Beyond

Our bereavement team stays connected for 13 months with counseling, support groups, and community resources. Your Bridge Hospice family does not disappear.
