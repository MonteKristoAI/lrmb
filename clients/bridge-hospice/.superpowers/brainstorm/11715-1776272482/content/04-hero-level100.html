Level 100 hero concepts for Bridge Hospice.

Concept A "Sacred Ground" — Full-viewport cinematic photo (elderly couple, hands held, warm light). 4-layer overlay system (radial vignette + directional gradient + color shift + warm tint). Film grain texture. Floating light orbs. Words reveal one by one with blur-to-sharp spring animation. Photo slowly zooms out on hover. Scroll indicator drips gold. Stats and programs below are part of the extended hero zone.

Concept B "Golden Hour" — Asymmetric split. Left: editorial typography with badge pills + quick access icons. Right: full-bleed photo with 5-layer fade system (left fade, top fade, bottom fade, vignette, gold tint overlay). A floating glass testimonial card slides in after 2.5s. Stats with animated gold underline on hover. Program cards reveal background photos on hover. Everything breathes.

Real Bridge Hospice photography replaces stock photos in production. All animations are live in this preview.
