### Our Story (Christopher-approved)

> For over 30 years, SDS has delivered rugged, universally compatible data acquisition systems to wireline operations across six continents. Our Warrior platform is the professional's standard — built on the principle that your surface system should never limit your downhole capabilities.
>
> We empower service companies to break free from closed, "black box" ecosystems. By providing a single platform that communicates with virtually any tool string, we give our customers the freedom to expand their service lines and compete at the highest level. We handle everything in-house — software, hardware, telemetry, and support. When you call us, you talk directly to the Houston-based engineers who designed your system.

### What We Stand For (Christopher-approved)

> Your crew should spend time running mission-critical operations — not troubleshooting equipment or fighting closed-system limitations. Every product we build prioritizes universal tool integration and field-validated reliability.
>
> We serve oil & gas, geothermal, environmental, and mining operations with systems proven in the most demanding well conditions on the planet, ensuring you capture every signal, from any tool, seamlessly.

### Extended Story (from Proposal Part 1)

> For 34 years, Scientific Data Systems has built wireline acquisition technology in Houston, Texas — not for a single manufacturer's ecosystem, but for the job itself.
>
> Our Warrior platform connects to virtually any downhole tool, from any manufacturer, through one surface unit and one unified workflow. Where other systems force you to choose sides, Warrior gives your crews the freedom to run the best tool for each job without changing panels, retraining operators, or second-guessing data quality.
>
> The new Universal Panel takes this further. Multiple switches, tool strings, and surface systems run simultaneously. Custom, job-specific interfaces mean each crew sees exactly what they need and nothing they don't. Automation handles the setup steps that used to depend on who was trained and who was watching — so your people can spend their time on safety and quality, not configuration.
>
> Our Warrior Data Logging Systems are manufactured and quality-controlled right here in Houston. From assembly to delivery, our engineers and technicians test every unit so the product is ready for immediate field use.
>
> We serve the petroleum, geothermal, and water well industries worldwide. Whether it's cased hole, open hole, slickline, memory, or LWD, Warrior is the central platform that ties it all together.
>
> We aren't just an oilfield tool. We are a resource management platform — powering the transition from oil to geothermal and beyond.
