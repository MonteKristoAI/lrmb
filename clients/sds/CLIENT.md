## What SDS Does

Scientific Data Systems builds the **Warrior** platform — a vendor-agnostic wireline data acquisition and control system. Warrior is the surface-side hardware + software that service companies run to log wells, perforate, and capture downhole telemetry, regardless of which manufacturer's tools they're running.

**The differentiator:** most competing systems (SLB, Halliburton, Weatherford) lock operators into a single tool ecosystem. Warrior connects to virtually any downhole tool from any manufacturer through one surface unit and one unified interface.

### Key numbers
- **~60%** of open-hole tools across manufacturers
- **~85%** of cased-hole tools
- **~90%** of addressable switches on the market
- **34 years** in Houston, manufactured in-house
- Serves 6 continents

### Current product launch: **Warrior Universal Panel**
NOT "Warrior 8.0". Warrior 8 (P10V7.1) is the existing software line. The launch is the new **Warrior Universal Panel (ULPP)** — hardware that consolidates multiple panels into a single unit, running multiple switches and tool strings simultaneously. Also introducing **Auto-Perf mode** for perforating stage firing.

### New pricing option
**Warrior leasing — $2,500/month** (addresses "too expensive to switch" objection). This is a prominent callout on the new site.
