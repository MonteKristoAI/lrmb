## Product

ProjectPro delivers **150-250 early-stage commercial construction projects** weekly to building material suppliers/distributors via Excel (Monday delivery before 9am). Each project includes architect, developer, and GC contacts with direct phone and email. Projects are 95% in planning or pre-construction stage (3-6 months before bid).

**Pricing (CAD)**:
- Western US: $299/mo (monthly), $199/mo (annual)
- Eastern US: $299/mo (monthly), $199/mo (annual)

**ICP**: VP Sales, Sales Directors, Regional Managers, Owners at commercial building material supplier/distributor companies in the United States.

## Campaign History

### V1-V3: "Cold Leads | Website | Free Trial" (Mar 10 - Apr 3, 2026) - INACTIVE
- 90 creatives across 3 ad sets at $70/day CAD
- Traffic sent to landing page then redirect to website for credit card registration
- **Results**: $871.75 CAD spent, 2 initiate_checkout events = $435.87/conversion
- **Killed because**: Too many creatives, too much friction, credit card ask too high for cold traffic

### V4: "ProjectPro | Leads | Website Lead | US | 1AS | v1" (Mar 27 - Apr 3, 2026) - TO BE PAUSED
- Consolidated to 1 ad set, 5 creatives per Brooks Van Norman coaching
- Still sending traffic to website for pixel lead event
- **Results**: $500.04 CAD spent, 2 lead events = $250/lead
- Best performer: Ad 04 "Reps Too Expensive" - 1 lead for $63.77, 4.59% CTR
- Worst: Ad 01 "Waiting for Bid Invites" - absorbed $277.95 for 1 lead

### V5: Native Lead Form Campaign (Apr 2026) - PLANNED
- **Strategy**: Native Meta lead form (no off-platform friction)
- **CTA**: "See if your team qualifies" (exclusivity framing)
- **Gating question**: "Does your company have its own outside sales team?"
- **Budget**: $70/day CAD, 15-20 days remaining (~$1,050-1,400)
- **Full strategy**: See `plans/harmonic-brewing-hummingbird.md` or strategy doc
