# NATIVE LEAD FORM - Level 10 Design

## Princip: Forma mora da se oseca kao APPLICATION, ne kao signup.

Kada neko "applies" za pristup, dozivljava veci perceived value
i veci commitment ka sledecim koracima. "See if you qualify"
framing psihicki stavlja korisnika u poziciju gde ZELE da budu
primljeni, umesto da osecaju da im se nesto prodaje.

---

## FORM TYPE: More Volume
(Na $70/day nemamo luksuza za Higher Intent. Trebamo volumen
da pixel nauci, a kvalitet kontrolisemo kroz gating pitanje.)

---

## INTRO SCREEN:

### Background Image:
Screenshot Excel tabele sa projektima (sa landing page-a, ili prava
anonymized tabela). Ovo ODMAH pokazuje sta dobijaju — konkretan
deliverable, ne abstraktno obecanje.

### Headline:
```
See If Your Sales Team Qualifies
```

### Body (max 2 recenice):
```
ProjectPro delivers 150-250 planning-stage commercial construction
projects to your inbox every Monday — with architect, developer,
and GC contacts. Trusted by suppliers for over 10 years.
```

### CTA Button Text:
```
Check Eligibility
```

**Zasto "Check Eligibility" a ne "Submit" ili "Sign Up":**
"Check Eligibility" implicira da postoji kriterijum i da mozda
nece svi biti primljeni. Ovo:
1. Povecava perceived value (ovo nije za svakoga)
2. Smanjuje perceived risk (samo proveravam, ne obavezujem se)
3. Povecava form completion rate (znatizeljan sam da li kvalifikujem)

---

## PITANJA (tacan redosled, tacna formulacija):

### Pitanje 1: First Name
- Type: Prefilled (from Facebook profile)
- Required: Yes

### Pitanje 2: Company Name
- Type: Short answer (custom question)
- Required: Yes
- Placeholder text: "e.g. ABC Building Supply"

**Zasto je Company Name DRUGO pitanje a ne trece:**
Korisnik je vec video svoje prefilled ime. Sada unosi kompaniju.
Ovo je nizak effort ali visoko kvalifikujuci — neko ko nema firmu
nece znati sta da upise i odustace. Prirodna kvalifikacija.

### Pitanje 3: Business Email
- Type: Email (custom, NOT prefilled)
- Required: Yes
- Placeholder text: "your@company.com"

**KRITICNO - Zasto NOT prefilled:**
Prefilled email je licni Gmail/Yahoo u 80% slucajeva.
Rucni unos business emaila:
1. Eliminise junk leadove (gmail.com, yahoo.com)
2. Daje nam company domain za verifikaciju
3. Povecava lead quality drasticno
4. Da, smanjuje form completion za ~15-20%, ali su leadovi
   koji ostanu 3-5x kvalitetniji

### Pitanje 4: Phone
- Type: Phone (prefilled from Facebook)
- Required: Yes

### Pitanje 5: Gating / Qualification Question
```
Which best describes your sales team?
```
- Type: Multiple choice (single select)
- Options:
  1. "We have outside sales reps covering territories"
  2. "Small team — 1-3 people handle everything"
  3. "We're building a sales team now"
  4. "It's just me"

**Zasto 4 opcije umesto 2 (Yes/No):**

Opcija 1 = GOLD lead. Ovo je ICP. Odmah phone call u roku od 4 sata.
Opcija 2 = SILVER lead. Mali tim ali imaju sales function. Email + phone.
Opcija 3 = BRONZE lead. Potencijal ali nije spreman. Email only.
Opcija 4 = DISQUALIFIED. Email only, low priority.

4 opcije daju:
- Bolju segmentaciju za follow-up (ne isti tretman svima)
- Bogatije podatke za pixel optimization (Meta moze da nauci ko su GOLD leadovi)
- Korisnik se oseca da mu se postavljaju legitimna poslovna pitanja, ne generic forma

### Pitanje 6 (OPCIONO - testirati sa i bez):
```
What does your team currently use to find commercial projects?
```
- Type: Multiple choice (single select)
- Options:
  1. "Dodge / ConstructConnect"
  2. "Bid boards and public sources"
  3. "Mostly relationships and referrals"
  4. "We don't have a system — it's ad hoc"

**Zasto ovo pitanje postoji:**
1. Segmentacija: Neko ko koristi Dodge za $3K/god je NAJVREDNIJI lead — vec placa za slicno, treba mu bolje/jeftinije
2. Sales intel: SDR zna tacno sta da kaze na telefonu ("I see you're on Dodge — here's how we're different...")
3. Copywriting intel: Odgovori nam govore koji pain point dominira (moze da usmeri sledecu iteraciju kreativa)

**NAPOMENA**: Ovo je 6. pitanje. Svako pitanje smanjuje completion rate
za ~5-10%. Testirati BEZ njega prvu nedelju, dodati u nedelji 2 ako
completion rate bude visok (>30%).

**Zasto "You're on the list" a ne "Thank you for submitting":**

- Implicira selekciju = veci perceived value
- "If your team qualifies" pojacava ekskluzivnost
- Stvara malu anksioznost: "Hoce li me primiti?" = veci engagement
  sa follow-up emailom
