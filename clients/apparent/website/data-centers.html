Proprietary Technology

eXeL Thermal Management

The eXeL system captures waste heat and converts it into usable hot water, reducing the thermal load on cooling systems. In data center environments, this translates to measurable reductions in HVAC energy consumption, lower PUE ratios, and extended equipment lifespan.

igOS Intelligent Load Balancing

igOS continuously monitors every energy flow in your facility, from server racks to cooling towers to battery backup. Its machine learning engine predicts demand spikes, optimizes power distribution across zones, and communicates with grid operators in real-time to secure the best energy rates.

Better Together

When eXeL and igOS work in concert, the thermal management feeds data to the intelligent load balancer, creating a feedback loop that optimizes both heating and electrical systems simultaneously. The result is a facility that runs cooler, cheaper, and greener.
