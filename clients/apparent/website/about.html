About Apparent

A different breed of energy company, built on patents, physics, and a refusal to accept the status quo.

Etymology

ap·par·ent

/uh-pair-uhnt/ | adjective

Electrical Engineering Definition

A term used in electrical engineering to describe apparent power in an alternating current system. Apparent power is the product of voltage and current in an AC circuit, measured in volt-amperes (VA), representing the total power flowing through the system.

We chose our name because it captures exactly what we do: we work with the full picture of how energy flows. While others focus on a single dimension, we see the complete power equation. Our technology manages apparent power across solar, grid, and storage systems, optimizing the total energy picture rather than just one piece of it.

Our Origin

From Bulgaria to Novato

A four-decade journey from nuclear engineering to the future of renewable energy.

1980s

The Beginning

A nuclear engineer in Bulgaria begins writing software to automate critical energy processes. This foundational work in understanding how energy flows through complex systems would become the intellectual bedrock of Apparent.

2000s

Breakthrough Invention

Decades of research culminate in the invention of a groundbreaking microinverter technology. By rethinking how energy is converted and managed at the component level, this innovation laid the groundwork for both the eXeL water heater and the igOS platform.

2011

Apparent Inc. Founded

Apparent Inc. is officially founded in Novato, California. The mission: leverage science to solve the world's energy problems. The company begins converting decades of research into commercial products backed by a growing patent portfolio.

2013

George Salah Joins as CEO

George Salah, a former Google executive who helped scale operations from 35 to over 50,000 employees, joins Apparent as Chairman and CEO. His operational expertise transforms the company from a research lab into a market-ready enterprise.

2020s

Scale & Impact

With 40+ patents secured, Apparent establishes partnerships with Google, Hawaiian Electric, BYD, and other industry leaders. The eXeL water heater reaches market, and igOS begins powering grid-edge intelligence for utilities and data centers nationwide.

Our Values

Innovate by Breaking the Model

We do not iterate on existing solutions. We question the fundamental assumptions behind them. Our patented microinverter technology emerged because we refused to accept that AC-only systems were the only path forward. When everyone else optimized within the box, we redesigned the box.

Keep It Simple

The most elegant engineering reduces complexity, it does not add it. eXeL installs like a standard water heater. igOS communicates with any system on the grid. We believe the best technology disappears into the background, doing its job without demanding attention.

Do It for Future Generations

Every decision we make is filtered through a simple question: will this matter in 30 years? We build products designed to outlast their warranties. We engineer efficiency that compounds over decades. Our goal is not to sell units, but to fundamentally change how energy works for the people who come after us.
