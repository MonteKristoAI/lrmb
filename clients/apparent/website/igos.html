What igOS Delivers

Grid Edge Intelligence

Autonomous, dynamic balancing of behind-the-meter generation and load in real-time. igOS makes intelligent decisions at the grid edge without centralized control latency.

Real-Time Energy Management

Sub-second bi-directional control to optimize available energy and use. Every watt is accounted for, routed, and managed with precision that traditional systems cannot match.

Digitization

Real-time data packets made available to grid operators for enhanced decision making. igOS transforms analog energy flows into actionable digital intelligence.

Flexible & Scalable

Plug-and-play capabilities enable addition of new resources without added costs. Whether you manage ten homes or ten thousand, igOS scales with your portfolio.
