Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

We are BYOB, so bring your own cooler with drinks (alcohol is welcome), snacks, or a picnic basket. We recommend sunscreen, sunglasses, and soft-soled shoes. Captain John provides all sailing equipment and safety gear.

Our classic sailboat accommodates up to 9 guests for a 2.5-hour cruise. For groups of 10-16, we offer a 3-hour extended cruise. For corporate team building, we can accommodate larger groups with multiple vessels and activities.

Safety is our top priority. If conditions are unsafe for sailing, Captain John will contact you to reschedule at no additional charge. Light rain typically does not cancel a trip and can create a beautiful, unique experience on the water.

We are at Safe Harbor Aqualand Marina, Dock Q, located at 6800 Lights Ferry Rd, Flowery Branch, GA 30542. It is about 40 minutes north of Atlanta, right in the most scenic part of Lake Lanier.

Not at all! Most of our guests are first-time sailors. Captain John and his crew handle everything. Of course, if you want to get hands-on and help raise the sails, you are more than welcome to.

Children are welcome on all cruises. We recommend ages 5 and up for the best experience. For sailing lessons, children 10 and older can participate with an adult. We also offer a special Pirate Cruise that kids absolutely love.

We recommend booking at least one week in advance, especially for weekend sunset cruises during summer months (June-August). Weekday availability is generally more flexible. For corporate events, 2-3 weeks notice is ideal.

Gratuity is not included in our charter prices but is always appreciated. Most guests tip 15-20% for exceptional service. Captain John and crew work hard to make every charter memorable.
