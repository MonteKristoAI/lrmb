Pricing Questions

We require a deposit to secure your reservation. The amount varies by charter type. Captain John will provide details when confirming your booking.

For private charters, if weather conditions are unsafe we reschedule at no charge. Wednesday's on the Water seats confirm Tuesday at 8:00 PM with a 4-seat minimum and move to the next Wednesday for weather or low-signup weeks (postponements only, no refunds).

Gratuity is not included in charter prices. Tips are appreciated and most guests tip 15-20% for great service.
