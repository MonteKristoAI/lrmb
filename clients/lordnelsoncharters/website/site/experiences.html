New · Group Sail

Wednesday's on the Water

A more accessible way to sail Lake Lanier and the only group sail we offer. Every Wednesday, up to ten guests share a 2.5-hour sail with Captain John for $79 per person. Bring your own drinks, meet the people, watch the sun drop. The first ten to sign up get the seats.

  • Group setting (not private) · 10 guests max
  • 2.5-hour Wednesday afternoon sail
  • BYOB welcome - coolers, drinks, snacks
  • Bumped to next Wednesday if weather cancels (no refunds)
  • Confirmed at Tuesday 8:00 PM once at least 4 seats are reserved
  • Subject to availability · private charters take priority
Reserve a Wednesday Seat

No charge until your sail is confirmed at Tuesday 8:00 PM. Postponements only, no refunds.

Most Popular

Sunset Sailing Cruises

There is nothing quite like watching the sun dip below the tree line while gliding across Lake Lanier. Our 2.5-hour sunset cruises depart approximately two hours before sundown, giving you front-row seats to nature's most spectacular light show. Bring your own wine, cheese, and good company. Captain John will handle the rest.

  • Private 2.5-hour cruise
  • BYOB welcome - coolers, wine, picnic
  • Perfect for couples, anniversaries, proposals
  • Departs from Safe Harbor Aqualand Marina
  • Best months: April through October

Classic Charter

Private Day Sails

Your own sailboat, your own crew, your own schedule. Private day sails range from 2.5-hour afternoon sails to full-day 7-hour adventures. Perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or just because you want to feel the wind in your hair and leave the city behind.

  • Choose 2.5-hour, half-day (4 hrs), or full-day (7 hrs)
  • Up to 9 guests per standard charter
  • Groups of 10-16 on extended 3-hour cruises
  • BYOB - pack a cooler and snacks
  • Hands-on sailing optional - learn the ropes!

Family Friendly

Family & Kids Cruises

Lake Lanier is a magical place for families, and there is no better way to experience it than from the deck of a sailboat. Pack a picnic, bring the kids, and let Captain John show you hidden coves, wildlife, and the simple joy of sailing together. Our special Pirate Cruise is a huge hit with kids.

  • All ages welcome (5+ recommended for best experience)
  • Pirate Cruise option with costumes and treasure hunt
  • Kid-friendly with safety equipment for all ages
  • Pack a picnic - BYOB for adults
  • Great for birthdays and family reunions
