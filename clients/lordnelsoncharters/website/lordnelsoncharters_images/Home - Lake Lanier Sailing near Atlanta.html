Welcome to Lord Nelson Charters

Nestled just 40 minutes from Atlanta, Lord Nelson Charters offers a unique blend of leisure and learning on the beautiful Lake Lanier. Our mission is to provide exceptional sailing experiences, whether you’re looking to unwind on a relaxing cruise or eager to learn the ropes with hands-on sailing. Led by the experienced Captain John Rice, our charters promise safety, enjoyment, and a touch of elegance. Join us to explore the lake’s beauty and discover the joys of sailing.
