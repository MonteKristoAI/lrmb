Capabilities

What DPROMPT Does

Five capabilities that turn field service dispatch from reactive chaos into proactive precision.

01

Technician Confirmation

Automatically confirm every technician before every job via AI-powered voice calls and SMS. No more manual phone trees or unanswered texts. Every confirmation generates structured data.

99%+ contact rate
< 4 min avg processing
02

No-Show Prevention

Detect risk signals early and escalate before failures impact customers. Pattern recognition across historical data flags potential issues 2+ hours before the arrival window.

40% reduction in no-shows
2+ hrs early warning
03

Reschedule Intelligence

When a technician cannot make it, DPROMPT surfaces the conflict, identifies the nearest qualified backup, and initiates the swap automatically. Zero manual dispatcher intervention required.

Auto backup matching
0 manual steps
04

Dispatch Escalation

Rule-based triggers notify dispatch teams when jobs are at risk. Set thresholds by job priority, geography, or client tier. Escalation paths route alerts to the right person every time.

Real-time alerts
Custom escalation rules
05

Operational Data

Every technician interaction becomes structured intelligence. Call outcomes, confirmation rates, response patterns, and reliability scores feed directly into your operational reporting.

100% data capture
API export ready
