Our Process

How We Work

Three phases. Zero surprises.

Phase 1

Assess

We analyze your deployment requirements, site conditions, and success criteria. This means understanding your hardware, your timeline constraints, and the specific nuances of each location. The output is a detailed project plan with staffing, logistics, and milestone commitments.

Phase 2

Deploy

AI-verified technicians execute with precision and real-time oversight. DPROMPT matches the right technician to the right site based on skills, proximity, and track record. Every step is documented with photos, timestamps, and digital sign-offs. You watch it happen live from your dashboard.

Phase 3

Optimize

Continuous improvement through operational data and AI insights. After every engagement, we analyze completion times, first-time-right rates, cost per site, and technician performance. Those insights feed back into future projects so each deployment we run is better than the last.
