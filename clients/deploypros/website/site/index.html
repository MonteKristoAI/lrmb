How It Works

From dispatch to deployment in three steps

Dispatch Sends Work Order

Your dispatch team submits work orders through your existing system. DPROMPT receives the assignment details, technician info, and schedule automatically via API or manual upload.

DPROMPT Orchestrates

The AI layer initiates confirmation calls, analyzes technician responses for risk signals, validates readiness, and flags potential no-shows before they happen.

Technician Confirms & Arrives

Confirmed technicians receive final details and arrive on schedule. Your team gets real-time status updates and structured outcome data for every deployment.

Average AI response time: Under 4 minutes
