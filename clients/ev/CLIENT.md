## What EV Does

EV builds **downhole video camera systems** and **visual analytics platforms** for the global energy industry. Their technology provides high-definition, real-time video from inside oil wells, gas wells, geothermal systems, and pipelines - environments with extreme temperature (200C+), extreme pressure (20,000 psi), and corrosive conditions.

**The core value proposition:** EV gives well operators "eyes on the job" - converting invisible downhole problems (corrosion, blockages, equipment failures, sand intrusion, leaks) into quantified, visual data that enables immediate remediation decisions.

### Key Differentiators
- **Only company** with integrated array video + phased array ultrasound (ClearVision technology)
- **25 years** of continuous innovation in downhole video
- **Visual Analytics (VA)** - proprietary computational analysis layered on video data
- **AIVA platform** - cloud-based asset inspection, visualization and analytics system
- **PigCAM** - free-swimming pipeline camera systems (4" and 8" variants, up to 176km range)
- In-house technology centers for R&D, manufacturing, and rapid prototyping
