Specialty Camera Solutions

Beyond the core Optis range, EV engineers purpose-built camera systems for specific operational challenges where standard tools fall short.

MagCam

Magnetic Carrier Camera

Combines a magnetic carrier mechanism with HD video for mini-fish extraction operations. The camera guides the magnetic tool to the target, providing visual confirmation of engagement before retrieval. Eliminates blind fishing attempts.

UVCam

UV Lighting Camera

Ultraviolet lighting system integrated with HD video for hydrocarbon identification. UV fluorescence makes hydrocarbons visible against pipe walls, enabling operators to locate active flow paths, identify water-oil contacts, and detect hydrocarbon ingress points.

VesselCam

Confined Space Inspection

Confined space inspection camera equipped with water jets for surface cleaning during inspection. Designed for pressure vessels, separators, and other confined equipment where surface contamination would otherwise obscure critical inspection data.

WedgeCam

Flapper Window Manipulation

Specialized camera system with integrated wedge mechanism for flapper window manipulation in multilateral completions. Provides visual guidance during window opening operations and confirmation of successful access to lateral wellbores.

Deployment

Conveyance Options

EV cameras deploy on any standard conveyance method, adapting to your existing rig-up and well access configuration.

Slickline

Memory-mode deployment on standard slickline. No surface data link required. All footage recorded downhole and retrieved at surface. Fastest mobilization for visual surveys.

Electric Line

Real-time surface video via electric line conveyance. Engineers see the wellbore live and can direct the camera to areas of interest during the run. Ideal for intervention planning.

Coiled Tubing

Camera deployment through coiled tubing for horizontal and deviated wells where wireline cannot reach. Enables visual inspection in well geometries that would otherwise be inaccessible.

Drill Pipe

Drill pipe conveyance for deep wells or wells under pressure where other methods are not practical. Provides reach and well control advantages for the most challenging deployments.

Analytics Software

Software & Analytics

Visual data is only valuable when it can be analyzed, quantified, and acted upon. EV's software platforms transform camera footage into engineering-grade intelligence.

AIVA

All-In-One Visualization and Analytics platform. EV's flagship software combines video playback, measurement tools, reporting, and AI-assisted analysis in a single desktop application.

Flagship Platform

MIPS

Industry-leading Multi-finger Caliper Processing Software. Processes multi-finger caliper data into detailed pipe condition assessments, wall thickness maps, and deformation profiles.

PIP

Pipe Integrity Platform. Comprehensive pipe condition database that tracks integrity data across multiple inspections, enabling trend analysis and remaining-life forecasting for well assets.

PDA

Pipe Deformation Analysis. Specialized analytical tool for quantifying casing and tubing deformation from visual and caliper data, producing 3D models of pipe geometry changes.
