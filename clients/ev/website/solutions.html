Visual Analytics Solutions

Our Visual Analytics services combine cutting-edge visual diagnostic information with advanced computational analysis to give you a high-definition, complete and quantified picture of your complex downhole challenges.

The VA Approach

From challenges such as severe scaling to pipe deformation, our game-changing Visual Analytics provide operators with an unparalleled understanding of the core reasons for downhole failure. Each VA service pairs high-definition camera footage with computational analysis to deliver quantified, actionable data that drives better decisions at the wellhead.

Perforation VA

Perforation Visual Analytics

The Challenge: Understanding cluster efficiency and perforation quality remains one of the most significant unknowns in completions engineering. Without direct visual evidence, operators rely on assumptions about perforation geometry, erosion patterns, and entry-hole quality.

The Solution: EV's Perforation VA delivers a complete quantified picture of every perforation in the wellbore, measuring size, shape, and erosion patterns with millimeter precision. The system captures 100% of perforations, enabling engineers to assess true cluster efficiency and optimize completion designs with hard data instead of guesswork.

Key Capabilities

  • 100% perforation capture across all stages
  • Cluster efficiency analysis with quantified metrics
  • Erosion quantification and pattern recognition
  • Completion design optimization recommendations

Corrosion VA

Corrosion Visual Analytics

The Challenge: Internal pipe corrosion is a silent threat to well integrity. Traditional caliper tools indicate material loss but cannot identify corrosion type, characterize severity visually, or distinguish between different degradation mechanisms.

The Solution: EV's Corrosion VA provides HD visual mapping of corrosion extent, type, and severity throughout the wellbore. By combining video with computational analysis, operators receive a complete corrosion profile that informs remediation strategies and remaining-life assessments.

Key Capabilities

  • Corrosion type identification (pitting, general, galvanic)
  • Severity grading with industry-standard classification
  • Wall loss estimation from visual measurement
  • Remediation planning with prioritized interventions

Fish VA

Fish Visual Analytics

The Challenge: Unknown objects blocking the wellbore (known as "fish") can halt operations for days. Without visual identification, fishing operations proceed blind, increasing costs, tool losses, and rig time.

The Solution: EV's Fish VA provides immediate visual identification and 3D characterization of any fish in the wellbore. Real-time footage gives fishing teams the critical information they need to select the right tool, plan the recovery approach, and execute on the first attempt.

Key Capabilities

  • Real-time fish identification and characterization
  • Dimensional analysis for tool selection
  • Recovery planning with visual evidence
  • Risk assessment and alternative approach modeling

Restriction VA

Restriction Visual Analytics

The Challenge: Reduced wellbore access can be caused by dozens of different mechanisms, from scale buildup to casing deformation. Without visual confirmation, operators run interventions based on assumptions that may be wrong.

The Solution: EV's Restriction VA delivers visual diagnosis with quantified restriction geometry. By seeing exactly what is causing the restriction and measuring its dimensions, engineers can select the optimal intervention method on the first attempt.

Key Capabilities

  • Visual restriction identification and classification
  • Geometry measurement (minimum ID, ovality)
  • Root cause analysis (scale, deformation, debris)
  • Intervention planning and tool recommendation

Valve VA

Valve Visual Analytics

The Challenge: Safety valve failures threaten well integrity and operational safety. Understanding why a valve has failed, whether it is a seal issue, corrosion, mechanical damage, or debris, determines the correct remediation path.

The Solution: EV's Valve VA provides direct visual inspection of downhole safety valves, revealing their true condition and failure mode. This removes the guesswork from valve diagnostics and enables targeted remediation instead of costly full replacements.

Key Capabilities

  • Failure mode identification (seal, corrosion, mechanical)
  • Seal condition assessment and wear analysis
  • Remediation guidance based on visual evidence
  • Valve function verification post-intervention

Water VA

Water Visual Analytics

The Challenge: Unwanted water entry is one of the most expensive problems in production operations. Water cut increases processing costs, reduces oil recovery, and can lead to premature well abandonment if the source is not identified and isolated.

The Solution: EV's Water VA visually pinpoints water sources within the wellbore. By identifying exactly where water is entering, operators can implement targeted isolation measures instead of broad-spectrum treatments that may reduce production from contributing zones.

Key Capabilities

  • Water entry localization with visual evidence
  • Source characterization (casing leak, formation, crossflow)
  • Production impact analysis and water cut modeling
  • Targeted remediation planning
View Case Studies
Leak VA

Leak Visual Analytics

The Challenge: Wellbore leaks pose serious safety, environmental, and production risks. Locating the exact leak point and understanding its characteristics are essential for planning effective repairs, yet traditional methods often lack the precision needed.

The Solution: EV's Leak VA delivers visual detection and characterization of leak points in the wellbore. Engineers can see the exact location, assess flow rate and severity, and plan repairs with confidence in the diagnosis.

Key Capabilities

  • Leak point identification with precise depth location
  • Flow characterization and rate estimation
  • Severity assessment for repair prioritization
  • Repair planning with visual evidence package
