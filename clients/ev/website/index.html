Visual Analytics Solutions

Nine purpose-built Visual Analytics solutions that solve the most complex downhole diagnostic challenges operators face globally.

Perforation VA

Evaluate perforation tunnel quality, identify blockages, and assess completion effectiveness with millimeter-precision visual data.

Corrosion VA

Detect and quantify tubular corrosion damage to guide repair decisions and extend asset life with visual evidence.

Fish VA

Locate and identify lost or stuck downhole equipment to plan precise fishing operations and reduce rig time.

Restriction VA

Identify and characterize wellbore restrictions including scale buildup, collapsed casing, and debris accumulations.

Valve VA

Verify downhole safety valve position, integrity, and functionality with direct visual confirmation under live well conditions.

Sand VA

Quantify sand production levels and assess screen condition to optimize sand management strategies across the field.

Plug VA

Verify plug placement, integrity, and cement quality during P&A operations with real-time visual confirmation.

Water VA

Detect water entry points and flow paths to diagnose unwanted water production and guide remediation efforts.

Leak VA

Pinpoint tubing and casing leaks with visual evidence to eliminate guesswork and reduce intervention costs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Visual Analytics is EV's proprietary methodology that transforms raw downhole video footage into structured, actionable diagnostic intelligence. Rather than simply recording images, our Visual Analytics process applies systematic evaluation frameworks to identify, classify, and quantify wellbore conditions such as corrosion, leaks, restrictions, and equipment failures. Each of our nine VA solutions is purpose-built for a specific diagnostic challenge.

Our camera systems are rated for operating pressures up to 20,000 PSI and temperatures exceeding 200 degrees Celsius. The Optis range, including the R150, M160, and Basis R200, are built to withstand extreme HPHT conditions routinely encountered in deep wells, geothermal reservoirs, and subsea completions. Every system is tested and certified before deployment.

Our downhole cameras can be conveyed via Slickline, Electric Line, Coiled Tubing, or Drill Pipe, depending on well conditions and operational requirements. This flexibility means EV systems integrate with your existing wireline or intervention spreads without requiring specialized surface equipment. Our field engineers coordinate conveyance planning with your operations team prior to every job.

AIVA stands for Asset Inspection Visualisation & Analytics. It is EV's cloud-based software platform where all Visual Analytics data is securely stored, analyzed, and shared. AIVA provides interactive 3D wellbore navigation, automated reporting, historical comparison tools, and role-based access control. Operators and engineers can access their survey data from any device, anywhere in the world.
