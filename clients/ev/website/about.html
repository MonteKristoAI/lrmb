About EV

Our Story

From Specialist Camera Company to Global Visual Analytics Leader

Founded around the turn of the millennium, EV has grown from a specialist downhole camera company into the world's leading visual analytics provider for the energy industry. Our journey has been defined by relentless innovation, deep technical expertise, and an unwavering commitment to helping energy operators see what was previously invisible.

What began as a focused effort to bring high-definition video into the wellbore evolved into something far more significant. By combining advanced camera technology with proprietary analytics software, EV created an entirely new category of diagnostic intervention: Visual Analytics.

Today, EV operates across six continents, serving major operators, national oil companies, and independent producers. Our technology portfolio spans nine dedicated Visual Analytics products, the AIVA software platform, and specialized hardware systems including the PigCAM series for pipeline inspection.

As part of the Enersol Group, EV has the resources and reach to continue pushing the boundaries of what's possible in energy diagnostics. From geothermal wells to subsea pipelines, from CCUS carbon capture projects to enhanced geothermal systems, EV's technology helps operators make better decisions, faster.

Our Commitment

Empowering Sustainability

We are committed to providing energy producers with the solutions they need to complete the energy transition, achieve carbon neutrality, and deliver sustainable energy.

Geothermal Energy

Our visual analytics technology is adapted for geothermal wells, supporting the development of both conventional and enhanced geothermal systems (EGS). We help operators characterize reservoirs, monitor well integrity, and optimize heat extraction.

CCUS Pipeline Assessment

PigCAM technology enables comprehensive assessment of legacy pipelines being repurposed for CO2 transport. Our visual inspection confirms pipeline integrity and cleanliness before conversion, accelerating CCUS project timelines.

Well Integrity

Maintaining well integrity is critical for reducing methane emissions and preventing environmental incidents. Our visual analytics identify leaks, corrosion, and damage before they become environmental liabilities.
