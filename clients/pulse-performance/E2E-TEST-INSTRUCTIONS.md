# Anna AI SDR - End-to-End Test Instructions

## What You Need
- Your GHL account (Pulse Performance)
- Your phone (to receive the call and SMS)
- Coupon code: **TESTMK** (so nothing gets charged to your card)

---

## Step 1: Create Your Test Contact

1. Go to **Contacts** in GHL
2. Click **+ Add Contact**
3. Fill in:
   - First Name: **Glenn**
   - Last Name: **Test**
   - Phone: **your real cell phone number**
   - Email: **your email**
4. Save the contact

---

## Step 2: Trigger the Call

1. Find your new "Glenn Test" contact in the contacts list
2. Click on the contact to open it
3. Click **+ Add to Workflow** (or go to the three dots menu and select **Add to Automation**)
4. Select the workflow: **FB Lead > Create Opportunity + Call**
5. Confirm

Anna (the AI SDR) will call your phone within a few seconds.

---

## Step 3: Talk to Anna

When Anna calls:

1. **Answer the phone** - Anna will introduce herself from Pulse Performance
2. She will ask what caught your eye in the ad - say something like **"I'm interested in EMS"**
3. She will ask about your goals - say **"I want to build muscle"**
4. She will ask what's been getting in the way - say **"No time"**
5. She will ask why now - say **"Summer is coming"**
6. She will pitch the $29 intro session - say **"Yeah, sounds good"**
7. **She will ask what day works for you** - pick a real day (e.g. "Thursday" or "tomorrow")
8. She will check the schedule and give you available times - **pick one**
9. She will say she is sending you a payment link

---

## Step 4: Pay with Test Coupon

1. After the call, **check your text messages**
2. You should receive an SMS with a **$29 payment link**
3. Open the link
4. Apply coupon code: **TESTMK**
5. Complete the payment ($0 after coupon)

---

## Step 5: Verify Booking

After payment, the system will automatically:

1. Book your EMS Intro Session in **ClubReady** for the time you chose
2. Send you a **confirmation SMS**: "You are booked! Your EMS Intro Session is confirmed for [time] on [date] at Pulse Performance Studio, 2286 Peachtree Rd, Atlanta. See you there!"
3. Update your opportunity in GHL to **"Intro Session Booked"**

---

## What to Check

After the test, verify:

- [ ] Anna called and had a natural conversation
- [ ] Anna asked what day you wanted before checking the schedule
- [ ] Anna checked real ClubReady availability (not made-up times)
- [ ] You received the $29 payment link SMS
- [ ] Coupon TESTMK worked
- [ ] You received a booking confirmation SMS after paying
- [ ] ClubReady shows the booking under your test contact
- [ ] GHL contact has custom fields filled in (fitness goal, challenge, etc.)
- [ ] GHL opportunity moved to "Intro Session Booked" stage

---

## What Happens If You DON'T Pay

If you skip the payment step:
- After **2 hours**, Glenn (you) will receive an email: "Payment Pending - Glenn Test"
- No ClubReady booking will be created
- The opportunity stays in "Working/In-Progress" stage

---

## Cleanup After Test

When done, delete:
- The "Glenn Test" contact from GHL
- The ClubReady record (search by your name in ClubReady admin)
- Any test opportunities in the pipeline
