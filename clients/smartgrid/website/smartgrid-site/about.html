Who Is SmartGrid

Your Single-Source Technology Partner

SmartGrid Integrations is a full-service IT systems integrator headquartered in Peachtree City, Georgia. Since 1996, we have been designing, deploying, and managing technology solutions for enterprises that demand reliability at scale.

From structured cabling to 24/7 managed services, we own every phase of the project lifecycle. Our clients trust us because we bring engineering depth, global reach, and a relentless focus on getting it right the first time.
