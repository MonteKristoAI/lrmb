Privacy Policy

How SmartGrid Integrations collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: April 2025

SmartGrid Integrations, Inc. ("SmartGrid," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to keep it safe.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information when you interact with our website, request a quote, or engage our services:

  • Contact details — name, email address, phone number, company name, and job title provided through our contact forms or direct communication.
  • Project information — technical requirements, site addresses, and scope details submitted during consultations or RFP processes.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent on our site, referral source, browser type, and device information collected automatically through cookies and analytics tools.
  • Communication records — emails, chat messages, and call logs related to service inquiries or ongoing projects.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide quotes for IT integration services.
  • Plan, coordinate, and deliver projects including structured cabling, network deployments, and managed services.
  • Improve our website, content, and user experience based on aggregate usage patterns.
  • Send relevant service updates or industry insights (you can opt out at any time).
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission via TLS/SSL on all web forms.
  • Access controls that limit data visibility to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis.
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure and third-party service providers.

While no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, we continuously evaluate and strengthen our safeguards to minimize risk.
