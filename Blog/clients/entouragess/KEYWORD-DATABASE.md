## Content Gaps (10 high-intent topics no competitor owns)

1. **"Edibles that actually feel like smoking a joint"** — Biggest single opportunity. Nobody in the field owns this positioning. Entourage Gummies is built for it.
2. **"Delta 9 gummies vs smoking" long-form** — SERP is thin; wide open for a data-rich comparison.
3. **Terpene profile + outcome matching** (limonene for creative work, linalool for sleep) — Generic across the category. No brand sells profile-matched gummies with matching content.
4. **State-by-state legality tracker updated for 2026** — Most ranking pages are 2023-2024. P-012 priority.
5. **Microdosing for performance / California sober** — Hemp brands have left this to mushroom brands. First-mover opportunity.
6. **"How to know if edibles are actually working"** — Zero brand coverage. Could be a linkable onset-time calculator tool.
7. **Fast-acting science explainers with original infographics** — Everyone explains nano-emulsification poorly. A definitive diagram becomes canonical.
8. **Occasion content** (gummies for concerts, dinner parties, hikes) — Long-tail wide open.
9. **"Hemp THC gummies that don't knock you out"** — Negative-framed search, uncovered.
10. **First-time user + low-dose SKU pairing** — Category over-indexes on 25mg "knockout" framing. Beginner path is open.
