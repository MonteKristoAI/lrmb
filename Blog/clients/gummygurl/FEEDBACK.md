# GummyGurl -- Editorial Feedback & Rules

## Standing Rules

1. **Zero medical claims.** Never say a product "treats", "cures", or "heals" any condition. Use language like "many users report" or "research suggests". FDA compliance is non-negotiable. (Pattern: industry standard, hemp/CBD regulatory requirement)
2. **Include compliance disclaimer on every post.** Full disclaimer text is in STYLE.md. Place at bottom of article before CTA. (Pattern: legal requirement)
3. **21+ language throughout.** Never reference minors, school settings, or underage scenarios. Products are for adults only. (Pattern: industry standard)
4. **Real prices, real product names.** Never use generic placeholders. Link to actual gummygurl.com product pages with current prices. (Pattern: E-E-A-T authenticity)
5. **November 2026 ban awareness.** Every post written after April 2026 should consider the regulatory timeline. THC-focused posts must acknowledge the upcoming change. (Pattern: time-sensitive, business-critical)
6. **COA references build trust.** Mention "third-party lab tested" and link to Lab Reports page at least once per product-focused post. (Pattern: E-E-A-T, industry best practice)
7. **No em dashes.** Use hyphens, commas, or rewrite. (Pattern: MonteKristo AI global rule)
8. **Blog is static HTML, NOT WordPress.** Blog posts go in the gummygurl-blog repo as HTML files. WordPress handles WooCommerce only. (Pattern: P-002, P-009)
9. **UTM tracking on all product links.** Format: `?utm_source=blog&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign={slug}` (Pattern: measurement)
10. **Phase 1 = Tier A only, 10 posts.** No Tier B or C in Phase 1. Depth over breadth. (Pattern: P-003)
11. **Auto-publish if no Seamus review in 72h.** 48h SLA is aspirational for a sole operator. If Seamus doesn't respond within 72h, publish non-health-claim posts automatically. Health/dosing posts require explicit approval (no auto-publish). This prevents the calendar from stalling on one person's inbox. (Pattern: L100 fix)
12. **Request real Seamus voice samples.** Before publishing post #2, collect 5+ emails/texts/product descriptions Seamus has written. Calibrate STYLE.md voice from actual speech patterns, not assumptions. (Pattern: L100 fix)
13. **Merge cannibalizing posts.** Never publish 2 posts targeting the same head keyword. If CONTENT.md has overlapping targets, merge into one definitive post. (Pattern: L100 fix)
