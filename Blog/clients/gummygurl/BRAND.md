# GummyGurl -- Brand Guidelines

## Colors

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Dark Navy | `#041122` | Primary text, headings, footer background |
| Brand Blue | `#007BFF` | Links, CTAs, accent elements, category labels |
| Cream | `#FAF8F4` | Page background, card backgrounds |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Content areas, cards |
| Text Dark | `#1D1F28` | Body copy |
| Muted | `#6b7d8e` | Secondary text, metadata, dates |
| Success Green | `#22c55e` | Key Takeaways border, positive indicators |
| Warning Amber | `#f59e0b` | Regulatory alerts, time-sensitive content |
| Error Red | `#ef4444` | Compliance warnings, ban countdown |

## Typography

- **Headings:** Poppins (600-700 weight)
- **Body:** Inter (400-500 weight)
- **Code/Data:** JetBrains Mono (for COA data, cannabinoid percentages)
- **Fallback stack:** system-ui, -apple-system, sans-serif

## Brand Voice Summary

GummyGurl is the knowledgeable, trustworthy friend who cuts through cannabis industry noise. The brand speaks with authority from hands-on production experience, backs claims with lab data, and treats every customer as an informed adult. The tone is warm but never preachy, enthusiastic but grounded in real testing and real prices.

## Key Differentiators

1. **Six specialized brands** under one roof (breadth no single-brand competitor can match)
2. **Every product lab-tested** with COAs publicly accessible (trust through transparency)
3. **In-house formulation and packaging** by Seamus (real production expertise)
4. **NC-based small business** (not a white-label reseller)
5. **Pet CBD line** (Nature Gurl draws a different audience than THC edibles)
6. **Mushroom crossover** (Rize + Lifted Labs position for post-ban pivot)
7. **First-mover on November 2026 hemp ban content** (regulatory authority)

## Logo

GummyGurl uses a custom wordmark. Logo files are in the main site assets. For blog use:
- Dark logo on cream backgrounds
- Light logo on dark/navy backgrounds
- Minimum width: 120px

## Age Gate

All blog pages must include a 21+ age verification reference. This is handled at the site template level (not per-post).
