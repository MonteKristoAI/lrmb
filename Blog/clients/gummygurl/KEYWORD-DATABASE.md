## Content Gaps (From Competitor Analysis)

1. **THCA flower strain-by-strain reviews** -- 3Chi does this thin, nobody does it deep
2. **November 2026 hemp ban explainer** -- nobody covering it comprehensively yet
3. **Pet CBD with breed-specific dosing** -- most guides are generic "by weight" only
4. **Mushroom + hemp product crossover** -- unique positioning, zero competitor content
5. **COA reading tutorial** -- 3Chi mentions COAs but nobody walks through reading one
6. **Delta-8 edible onset/duration data** -- most posts are vague, opportunity for specifics
7. **State-by-state legality** -- existing guides are outdated (pre-2026 regs)
8. **Subscription/savings content** -- competitors ignore recurring revenue angle
9. **Behind-the-scenes production** -- nobody shows how products are actually made
10. **Multi-cannabinoid comparison** (CBD vs CBN vs CBG vs Delta-8 vs Delta-9 vs THCA) -- nobody has a single comprehensive comparison
