# LuxeShutters — Editorial Feedback & Rules

## Standing Rules

1. **Australian English only.** Colour, metre, aluminium, louvre. Never American spellings.
2. **Zero em dashes.** Use commas, colons, or split the sentence. Global MonteKristo rule.
3. **Real product names.** Use CWGlobal product names (Forte, Bayview, Oasis, Atlas, Aura Drapery) where relevant. This signals dealer expertise.
4. **No fake urgency.** No "limited time" or "don't miss out" language.
5. **Pricing = installed prices.** Publish what homeowners pay (with markup + installation), NOT dealer cost from CWGlobal. Always include freshness date and disclaimer.
6. **Every post gets a Riverina section.** Even non-local posts need 100-150 words of regional context.
7. **No stock photos for case studies.** Case study posts MUST use real installation photos or don't publish.
8. **Answer first, explain second.** Every H2 opens with a direct answer, then provides supporting detail.
9. **Structural variation is mandatory.** Never use the same post template for 3+ consecutive posts. See STYLE.md template types.
10. **Target national keywords, localise through content.** "plantation shutters cost australia" as keyword, Riverina context in body. NOT "plantation shutters cost riverina" (zero volume).
11. **Every post checked against Chris Voice Corpus.** "Could Chris have plausibly said this?" If not, rewrite.
12. **GBP post for every blog post.** Repurpose each blog post as a 150-300 word Google Business Profile post summary with link.
13. **Pricing posts never overlap products.** Post #1 = shutters only. Post #7 = blinds/curtains/other. FAQ page answers are 1-2 sentences with link to full post, never full duplicates.
