# LuxeShutters — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Colors

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Primary Blue | #1E5AA8 | Headings, CTAs, links |
| Dark Navy | #0F172A | Body text |
| Light Background | #F8FAFC | Page background |
| White | #FFFFFF | Cards, content areas |
| Accent Gold | #D4A853 | Premium highlights (sparingly) |

## Typography

- **Headings:** System font stack (matches website)
- **Body:** System font stack
- **Code/data:** Monospace for pricing tables

## Logo

Located at: `LuxeShutters_website/src/assets/logo-black.webp` (dark backgrounds: `logo-white.webp`)

## Brand Voice Summary

- Professional but approachable
- Knowledgeable tradesperson, not corporate marketer
- Direct, specific, Australian
- Proud of local roots (Temora, Riverina)
- Product expert who names specific models and materials
- Honest about pricing and limitations

## Tagline / Positioning

"Premium Shutters, Blinds & Curtains — Temora and the Riverina, NSW"

## Key Differentiators (Use in Content)

1. **Local, not national.** We live and work in the Riverina.
2. **CWGlobal dealer.** Australian-manufactured products.
3. **Measure to install.** Same team does everything, no subcontractors.
4. **Full product range.** 17+ product types under one roof.
5. **Free in-home consultation.** No obligation, no pressure.
6. **Real pricing transparency.** We publish what things actually cost.
