## FAQ (5+)

1. **How much does Warrior leasing cost?** Warrior leasing is priced at $2,500 per month under the 2026 Scientific Data Systems leasing program. The monthly price includes the Warrior hardware, software, support, and updates. The actual contract structure and any configuration upcharges require direct contact with SDS in Houston.

2. **What's the typical capex for a new wireline surface system?** Wireline surface systems from major manufacturers are not publicly priced but typically run between $250,000 and $1 million for a fleet-ready deployment. The variation depends on fleet size, included tools, software licenses, and support contracts. Warrior's capex purchase path exists alongside the leasing option for operators who prefer ownership.

3. **When does leasing beat buying?** For small to mid-size wireline service companies (fewer than 15 trucks), Warrior leasing typically beats capex purchase over a 36-60 month window because leasing eliminates upfront capital requirements, consolidates ongoing costs, and avoids end-of-life equipment disposition costs. Larger fleets may find capex purchase more economical after roughly 48 months depending on utilization.

4. **What's included in the $2,500/month lease?** The Warrior leasing program includes the hardware (surface panel and accessories), the Warrior 8 software suite, engineering support, and software updates. Training for new crews and consumables (cables, connectors) are separate. The exact scope is defined in the lease contract with SDS in Houston.

5. **Is the lease fully cancelable?** Contract cancellation terms depend on the specific lease agreement with SDS. Standard practice in capital equipment leasing is a minimum term (often 36 months) with early-termination provisions. Contact SDS for the specific terms that apply to your fleet and contract structure.

6. **Does leasing affect support quality or priority?** No. SDS's support model is the same whether a customer buys or leases — engineering support comes directly from the Houston team that built the platform. The leasing program does not create a separate tier; a leasing customer has the same access to Warrior's engineering and field support as a capex purchaser.
