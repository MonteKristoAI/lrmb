### Cross-post strategy per blog post

For every launch post, create these LinkedIn assets (generated by `/blog repurpose`):

## 3. SPE Connect + OnePetro Distribution

SPE Connect is the Society of Petroleum Engineers' discussion and community platform. It is where the actual technical audience hangs out.

### For each launch post
1. **Identify the relevant SPE Technical Community** (Formation Evaluation, Completions, Production & Operations)
2. **Post a discussion-starter** (NOT a link drop) — 150-250 word technical question or hypothesis, link to the blog post as "further reading" at the end
3. **Engage with replies** — Christopher is the credible voice here; ideally he responds to every substantive comment within 24 hours

### OnePetro contribution strategy (long-term)
- **Convert 2-3 launch posts into conference paper submissions** for SPE ATCE 2026 or URTeC 2027
- **Priority candidates:** Post #3 (addressable switch comparison) and Post #5 (API RP 67 4th edition primer) — both are technical enough to warrant a formal paper

## 10. AI Citation "Distribution"

AI citation is passive distribution — once content is indexed, it surfaces on queries without action. But it requires structural optimization (see `CITATIONS.md`).

### Specific tasks
1. **Submit the blog sitemap** to Google Search Console, Bing Webmaster Tools, and (if available) Perplexity / ChatGPT crawlers
2. **Create `llms.txt`** at the blog root (see `CITATIONS.md` §8)
3. **Monitor** with `/blog geo` monthly for each post
4. **Publish in clusters** — AI crawlers prefer content that sits inside a linked topic cluster over standalone posts

## 14. What We're NOT Doing

- **Not paying for ads on launch posts** — organic first, measure, then decide
- **Not blasting LinkedIn Groups with direct links** — we contribute value first
- **Not writing SDS-branded content that reads like a press release** — editorial voice always
- **Not auto-posting** — every share has a human touch
- **Not chasing viral topics outside the wireline niche** — audience-first, not clicks-first
