## Status Definitions

- `planned` — brief exists, not yet in writing
- `writing` — in progress (wip in blog-progress.json)
- `review` — written, awaiting quality gates
- `published` — live on site
- `refresh` — queued for quarterly refresh
- `archived` — pulled from site, no longer live
