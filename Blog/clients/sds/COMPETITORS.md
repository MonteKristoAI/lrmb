# SDS / Warrior — Competitive Content Analysis

**Purpose:** Identify content gaps SDS can own. For every competitor, we note what they cover, what they can't cover (because of business-model constraints), and the specific angle SDS should exploit.

**Core insight:** The 4 majors (SLB, Halliburton, Weatherford, Baker Hughes) cannot write honestly about vendor-agnostic wireline because each sells a closed ecosystem. The perforating switch manufacturers (Dyna, Titan, G&H) cannot write honestly about cross-manufacturer compatibility because each wants you to standardize on their switch. **SDS is structurally positioned to tell truths competitors cannot.**

---

## Tier 1 — Direct Surface Acquisition System Competitors

### 1. SLB / Schlumberger — MAXIS (Multitask Acquisition and Imaging System)
- **Their content surface:** slb.com/products-and-services/innovating-in-oil-and-gas/reservoir-characterization/surface-and-downhole-logging/
- **Dominant content format:** product brochures, case study PRs, technical papers behind OnePetro
- **What they do well:** deep technical authority, SPE paper volume, brand weight, global SERP dominance on generic terms
- **What they structurally cannot say:** "run any manufacturer's tool", "we work with competitor equipment", "open platform"
- **Their SEO signals:** brand + technical depth; limited answer-first formatting; rare FAQ schema
- **Gap SDS can own:**
  - Multi-vendor tool string guides (SLB will always push SLB tools)
  - Honest cost comparisons (SLB never discusses price)
  - Switching-cost runbooks (SLB is the lock-in, not the escape)

### 2. Halliburton — InSite wireline suite
- **Their content surface:** halliburton.com/en/subsurface/formation-evaluation
- **Dominant format:** product landing pages, case study PDFs, thought-leadership whitepapers

- **Structural blind spot:** same as SLB — cannot honestly write vendor-agnostic content
- **Gap SDS can own:**
  - "How to integrate Halliburton tools into a non-Halliburton surface platform" — direct capture intent
  - Independent production logging comparison

### 3. Weatherford — Wireline Surface Equipment **(+ SDS Warrior distributor)**
- **Their content surface:** weatherford.com/products-and-services/formation-evaluation/wireline-services/wireline-surface-equipment
- **Unique position:** **Weatherford distributes the SDS Warrior panel.** Confirmed at [weatherford.com/products-and-services/drilling-and-evaluation/wireline-products/telemetry-systems/scientific-data-systems-warrior-well-logging-system-panel/](https://www.weatherford.com/products-and-services/drilling-and-evaluation/wireline-products/telemetry-systems/scientific-data-systems-warrior-well-logging-system-panel/)

### 4. Baker Hughes — CIRRUS wireline control system

## Tier 3 — Industry Publications (to beat for informational intent)

### 11. Journal of Petroleum Technology (JPT) — jpt.spe.org
- **Content pattern:** technology updates, conference highlights, SPE paper digests
- **Strong content on:** DynaStage safety story, Fervo FORGE geothermal updates, high-temp tool R&D
- **Gap SDS can own:** JPT writes for SPE members — technical and academic. SDS should write for the *operator* in the field. Same topic, different voice and format (practitioner-focused vs analytical).
- **Distribution opportunity:** SDS blog posts can be pitched to JPT as condensed technology updates.

### 12. World Oil — worldoil.com
- **Content pattern:** industry news, operator case studies
- **Gap:** weak on wireline surface system technology (most coverage is downhole tools)
- **SDS opportunity:** be the source they cite for Universal Panel coverage

### 13. Hart Energy / E&P — hartenergy.com
- **Content pattern:** broad upstream coverage
- **Sample:** [Alleviating Firing System Safety Risks And Costs](https://www.hartenergy.com/exclusives/alleviating-firing-system-safety-risks-and-costs-177249/) — covers addressable switches generically
- **Gap:** no deep wireline surface system coverage
- **Opportunity:** contribute or pitch a Universal Panel thought-leadership piece

### 14. Oil & Gas Journal (OGJ), Rigzone, Offshore Engineer, Drilling Contractor, Upstream Online
- **Pattern:** all cover wireline as a sub-topic, rarely deep
- **Gap:** nobody owns the "wireline surface system buyer's guide" niche

### 15. SPE OnePetro
- **Content:** paywalled technical papers
- **SDS presence:** should submit papers on Universal Panel architecture to SPE ATCE
- **Gap:** academic, not practitioner-friendly — SDS blog is the free layer that links to OnePetro
