## Who They Are

Renewable Energy Integration Group (REIG) is a **solar SCADA and DAS integration contractor** that delivers end-to-end design, installation, commissioning, and ongoing support for utility-scale solar PV assets. Founded in 2016 in Charlotte, NC.

REIG integrates monitoring, controls, and communications — including fiber/optical services — to bring systems online faster, reduce downtime, and keep plant data reliable from commissioning through operations. Their role in any project is consistently the specialist SCADA/DAS/fiber subcontractor within EPC-led builds.

Their own product line, **RenergyWare**, is a series of rugged, field-proven NEMA 4 / UL-listed enclosure packages designed for utility-scale solar monitoring, DAS, and SCADA communications.

**Mission:** *"Turn renewable energy data into dependable decisions — so your team can maximize production, shorten troubleshooting, and protect ROI."*

## Target Audience (ICP)

Utility-scale solar **owners/operators, developers, EPCs, and O&M teams** who need SCADA + DAS systems commissioned correctly and fast.

**Specific buyer roles addressed in blog content:**
- Project managers and commissioning leads
- SCADA/DAS engineers
- Asset managers responsible for COD readiness
- Grid/utility interface engineers
- O&M teams responsible for ongoing performance monitoring

**Their pain points (from blog analysis):**
- Plant comes "online" but data isn't trustworthy
- "Mystery data gaps" that only appear post-COD
- SCADA shows one thing, historian shows another ("online but wrong")
- Poor documentation leading to post-COD rework
- Fragmented handoffs between fiber crews and SCADA teams
