## Anchor Text Rules

Never use generic anchor text ("click here", "read more", "this post"). Always use descriptive text.

### Good examples
- "Dan covers the [three patterns that calm the body fastest](/breathing-techniques-for-anxiety-the-3-patterns-that-calm-the-body-fastest/) in a companion post."
- "The underlying [science of coherent breathing and the thoracic pump](/coherent-breathing-and-the-thoracic-pump/) explains why this works."
- "If you want a structured introduction, the [21-Day Breathing Training](https://breathmastery.com/21daytraininginbreathwork/) is free."

### Bad examples
- "[Read this post](...)."
- "[Click here](...)."
- "[Learn more](...)."
