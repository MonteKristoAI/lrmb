## Who They Are

Breath Mastery is a breathwork education and personal transformation brand built entirely around **Dan Brulé** — a former US Navy medical deep-sea diver, CPR instructor (personally resuscitated 19 people), X-ray technologist, and Master's degree holder in the Healing Arts. Since 1970, he has trained more than **300,000 people in 73–75 countries** and holds the full lineage of the modern breathwork field, having studied with Leonard Orr (Rebirthing), Stan Grof (Holotropic Breathwork), Swami Rama (Prana Yoga), and Master Hu Bin (Medical Chi Kung, Beijing Academy of Chinese Medicine).

The brand bridges science and spirituality without apology — it is neither purely functional breathwork nor purely spiritual. Dan describes himself as a "missionary for the breath" and leads the worldwide **Spiritual Breathing Movement**. His personal breathing coach clients include **Tony Robbins**, who wrote the foreword to Dan's book.

**Founded:** One Sky International (1990); Breath Mastery brand active since at least 2015

## Core Philosophy (The Frameworks)

### Two Pillars (Central to Everything)
1. **Breath Awareness** — passive, observing the breath without controlling it; mindfulness of breath
2. **Conscious Breathing** — active, directing the breath with intention; "taking charge"

### The Formula for Transformation
1. **Awareness** = "the consciousness factor" → "Wake up!"
2. **Relaxation** = "the release factor" → "Let go!"
3. **Breathing** = "the energy factor" → "Take charge!"
Core mantra: **"Open and expand. Relax and let go."**

### The Eight Paths (Used in Breath Tech App)
Body Wise · Heart Intelligence · Business Edge · Go for the Gold · Be Your Best · Making Magic · Helping the Helper · Soul Source

### The Three Breathing Spaces
- Lower (perineum to belly): body intelligence, instinct, grounding
- Middle (belly to nipple): heart intelligence, intuition, connection
- Upper (nipple to chin): mental energy, intellect, higher intelligence
