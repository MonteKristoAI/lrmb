## Unique Angle / Positioning

Breath Mastery is the only breathwork education brand that bridges science and spirituality without compromise, grounded in 50+ years of direct lineage from the founders of the modern breathwork field (Rebirthing, Holotropic, Prana Yoga, Medical Chi Kung). Dan Brulé is not a wellness influencer — he is a former US Navy medical deep-sea diver, CPR instructor who personally resuscitated 19 people, and the teacher who trained Tony Robbins in breathwork.

The brand occupies a unique position: **serious enough for medical professionals and Olympic athletes; accessible enough for complete beginners; spiritual enough for seekers without being exclusive to any tradition.**

**The core promise:** Breath Awareness + Conscious Breathing. Passive observation + active direction. The simplest possible path to the most profound possible transformation.

1. **Breath Awareness** — passive; observing/tracking the breath without controlling it; the mindfulness side

Together, these two pillars form the complete practice. Every blog post should relate back to one or both.

## Off-Limits Topics

- **No competitive takedowns** — do not name or compare against Wim Hof Method, Oxygen Advantage, Transformational Breath, or any other system. Differences are shown by making Dan's depth vivid, not by diminishing others. (Wim Hof is explicitly a peer and endorser.)
- **No medical claims** — never say breathwork "cures," "treats," or "heals" diagnosed conditions. Use: "may support," "can complement," "has been shown to." Note contraindications (pregnancy, cardiovascular conditions, seizure history, serious mental illness).
- **No religious gatekeeping** — Dan bridges Hindu, Buddhist, Taoist, Christian, Sufi, and shamanic traditions. No post should make any tradition primary or exclusive.
- **No intensity pressure** — never encourage pushing through discomfort, "warrior breathing," or competing with oneself. Dan is explicitly anti-force: "regulation beats intensity," "gentleness usually takes you further."
- **No Dan Brulé personal scandals** — Dan wrote publicly about personal missteps in 2023 (professional ethics, boundaries). Do not reference in blog posts.
- **No pseudoscience claims without grounding** — pineal gland activation, chakra clearing, etc. may appear in posts but must be framed as experiential/traditional context, not medical fact.
- **No keyword-stuffed headings** — Dan and his website manager Paul Taubman explicitly flagged this. H2/H3 headings must guide readers logically, not repeat the H1 keyword. *(Source: March 2026 editorial feedback)*
- **No overloaded focus on pineal gland** — Dan explicitly said "the pineal gland is not a major thing for me." It's a supporting SEO topic; don't build posts around it as a core Dan Brulé teaching. *(Source: Dan's direct email, March 2026)*

## Legal / Compliance Notes

- **Medical disclaimer required** in every post discussing anxiety, depression, PTSD, trauma, or physical health conditions: *"Breathwork is not a substitute for professional medical advice. If you have a health condition, consult a qualified healthcare provider before beginning a breathwork practice."*
- **Safety note required** in any post featuring powerful or rapid breathing techniques (Bhastrika, connected breathing, Holotropic-style): include contraindications — pregnancy, cardiovascular conditions, epilepsy/seizure history, recent surgery, serious mental illness requiring stabilization.
- **Do not claim diagnoses** or promise specific outcomes. Use: "research suggests," "has been shown to support," "many practitioners report."
- **Attribution:** When citing studies, include the organization name and publication or DOI.
